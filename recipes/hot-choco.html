Hot Choco - A Filipino Sikwate Recipe

Hot choco or Sikwate is by its name suggests a hearty drink of dark chocolate. This drinks soothes the soul with its rich, flavorful, and natural chocolate goodness, which is perfect for rainy days and cold weather.

Ingridients

  • Tablea or Cocoa
  • Water
  • Sugar

Additional Ingridients

  • Milk: Liquid or Powdered

Equipment

  • A pot
  • A laddle
  • A mug

Steps

  1. Boil water, the amount depends on the tablea you use. 1 tablea is usually for 2 cups
  2. If water is on a boil, lower the heat and add the desired amount of tablea.
  3. Cook for around 5 minutes or more while stirring so the tablea doesn't burn.
  4. Pour and serve it in a mug
  5. Add sugar, milk, or other compatible ingridients to your cup to taste.
